SpoopyGame

SpoopyGame is a procedurally generated dungeon crawler/puzzle game. I helped create it as part of a group project for CMPT 306 - Game Mechanics with three other members. All of the dungeons are proceduarally generated along with the placement of puzzles and monsters. The player must survive as many levels as they can, and solve puzzles to gain powerups and potions. As the game progresses, the dungeons get more complicated.

I created all of the art for this game, as well as music and the user interface. I also helped to ensure puzzles were solvable once they were placed in the map.
