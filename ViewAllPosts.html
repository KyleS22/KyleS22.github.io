Ten Plus Ten Sketching

  • Sketching
  • Design
  • Creative Thinking

As part of my degree requirements I had to take CMPT 481: Human Computer Interaction. The class focuses on the design process for creating effective user interfaces. One of the most useful things I learned in that course was 10 plus 10 sketching, a technique for quickly coming up with new design ideas, without having to commit to one particular design. Design has always been difficult for me as I find it difficult to find good ideas. Then when I do have a good idea it gets stuck in my head as 'THE' idea to use.
